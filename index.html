About Us

Tree planting is the process of transplanting treeseedlings, generally for forestry,land reclamation, or landscaping purpose. It differs from the transplantation of larger trees in arboriculture, and from the lower costbut slower and less reliable distribution of tree seeds.
Our goal is to expand our successful model that we pioneered in India and expand it globally to improve living standards, reduce global warming and save and plant more and more trees every year.
